# 🚀 Marketing Performance Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is a **Marketing Performance Analytics Dashboard** built to analyze advertising campaigns and evaluate business performance using real-world KPIs.
The goal is to transform raw marketing data into actionable insights that support **data-driven decision making** and improve profitability.

---

## 🧠 Business Problem

Despite generating high traffic and conversions, the business was operating at a loss due to inefficient ad spending and poor campaign optimization.

Key challenges:

* Low profitability despite high conversions
* Inefficient budget allocation across campaigns
* High cost per acquisition (CPA)
* ROAS below 1 for most campaigns

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze marketing performance across multiple dimensions
* Identify profitable vs unprofitable campaigns
* Understand customer behavior across devices and channels
* Optimize advertising spend based on ROI
* Improve business decision-making using data

---

## 🗂 Data Modeling

A **Star Schema** was designed to structure the data efficiently.

### Fact Table:

* Marketing performance metrics (Clicks, Conversions, Cost, Revenue)

### Dimension Tables:

* Campaigns
* Date
* Devices
* Ad Types
* Channels

✔ This structure improved:

* Query performance
* Data consistency
* Scalability
* Ease of analysis in Power BI

---

## 🎨 Dashboard Features

* Multiple views for:

  * Revenue analysis

  * Conversion performance
  * ROAS breakdown

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Overall ROAS = **0.88**, indicating unprofitable ad spending
* Coupon & Promo campaigns generated high conversions but negative profit
* Black Friday campaigns achieved exceptional ROAS (4.4) but were underfunded
* November showed high demand but poor profitability due to high costs
* Mobile generated higher revenue, while desktop users were more profitable
* 1:1 campaigns drove most conversions but caused the largest losses
* October was the only profitable month (ROAS > 1)

---

## 📌 Recommendations

* Reallocate budget toward high-performing campaigns (e.g., Black Friday)
* Reduce spending on unprofitable campaigns (Coupon, 1:1)
* Improve mobile user conversion experience
* Focus on profitability, not just conversion volume
* Analyze October strategy and replicate success factors
* Optimize CPA and CPC to improve overall ROAS
